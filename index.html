What is Diversity and Inclusion

Definition of diversity

Diversity in the workplace refers to the differences among people in an organisation, including race, gender,age, sexual orientation and more.

Definition of Inclusion

Inclusion involves creating a supportive environment where everyone feels valued and accepted.

Why Diversity and Inclusion matter

Statistics: Diverse workplaces see improved innovation and higher employee satisfaction.

Impact on performance: Diversity enhances team performance and creativity, leading to better problem-solving and decision making.
